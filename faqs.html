Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about iVvy's venue management platform.

What is iVvy?

iVvy is a cloud-based event and venue management platform that helps venues streamline operations, manage bookings, and grow revenue. It's designed for hotels, function centres, stadiums, restaurants, universities, and government venues.

How does iVvy help my venue?

iVvy provides tools for CRM, online booking, quotes and contracts, function diary, BEO generation, billing, reporting, and integrations — all in one platform. This eliminates manual processes and helps your team work more efficiently.

Is iVvy suitable for small venues?

Yes! iVvy scales to fit venues of all sizes, from boutique hotels to large stadium complexes and multi-property groups.

What integrations does iVvy support?

iVvy integrates with 55+ tools including PMS systems (Opera, Mews), accounting software (Xero, MYOB, QuickBooks), payment gateways, and more. Visit our integrations page for the full list.

How secure is my data with iVvy?

Very secure. We use enterprise-grade encryption, comply with GDPR and PCI DSS, and maintain SOC 2 compliance. Visit our security page for details.

Can I get a demo?

Absolutely! Request a demo and our team will walk you through the platform and show you how it can work for your venue.

Where can I get support?

Our support team is available via our Knowledge Base. We also provide dedicated account management for all customers.
