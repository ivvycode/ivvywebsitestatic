About

We are passionate about events

At iVvy, we are passionate about transforming the venue and event management industry. Our cloud-based venue and event software solutions empower venue operators, event organisers, and suppliers to streamline operations, increase bookings, and enhance overall efficiency.

Our story

Venues know how to deliver memorable events. The challenge has always been finding the time, having a clear picture of what's available, and keeping teams on track with limited resources.

iVvy was founded in 2009 on the Gold Coast to solve that challenge. From day one, our goal has been to help venues respond to enquiries faster, see their business more clearly, and reduce the manual work that slows venue & event teams down.

What began as a startup has grown into a global platform, used by more than 10,000 people across 13 countries. With offices in Australia, South Africa, the USA, and the UK, we continue to help venues turn leads into bookings, make confident decisions with real-time data, and run events more efficiently.

What we do

Our cloud-based venue and event management software helps venues of all sizes:

  • Win more bookings with built-in marketing tools and live availability.
  • Stay in control with centralised data, contracts, and communications in one place.
  • Make smarter decisions with real-time reporting and forecasting.
  • Free up time by automating repetitive admin.

TEAM

Meet the executive team

iVvy is led by an experienced executive team with backgrounds in SaaS, hospitality, and technology, and a proven track record of scaling businesses globally. Together, their focus is on building practical, data‑driven solutions that support day‑to‑day operations for event and venue management teams.

Lauren Hall Founder & Chief Executive Officer
Lauren Hall founded iVvy in 2009 with a clear vision to simplify the way venues and event professionals manage enquiries, availability, and bookings.

As Founder and CEO, she has grown iVvy from a start-up into a multi-award-winning global SaaS company serving venue and event teams across 13 countries. Lauren sets the company's strategic direction and remains closely involved in product innovation.

A passionate entrepreneur and accomplished executive, Lauren brings extensive domestic and international experience across technology, retail, manufacturing, and advertising. With a background spanning programming, accounting, and marketing, she has built and scaled multiple businesses from start-up through to financial and strategic exit.

Lauren is widely recognised, receiving multiple international awards for entrepreneurship and product excellence. She is driven by a deep commitment to building high-performing teams, delivering sustainable growth, and creating long-term value for customers and shareholders.
