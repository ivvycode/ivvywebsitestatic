What is Billing Automation?

Billing Automation is a time-saving feature that works with iVvy Pay to take the manual work out of invoicing. You can set up recurring invoices to go out automatically on a schedule that fits your venue's needs. Each invoice includes a 'Pay Now' button, making it easier for customers to pay on time. By cutting down repetitive admin, your team can save up to 10 minutes per booking.
