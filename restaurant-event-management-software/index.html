Event software for restaurants & catering companies

For restaurants and catering businesses, managing venue bookings, menus, and communication often means juggling multiple systems and manual tasks.

iVvy simplifies it all. Our Venue & Event Management Software brings everything into one platform, so your team can respond faster, work smarter, and deliver smoother events.

With iVvy, your team can:

  • Manage bookings and catering orders from one platform
  • Customise menus and packages for every event
  • Track inventory and resources in real time
  • Access live data to make informed decisions

Restaurants and catering companies using iVvy create quotes 80% faster and book 25% more events.
