FAQs

Frequently asked questions

What is iVvy, and what does it do?
iVvy is cloud-based venue and event management software that helps venues manage bookings, events, and customer relationships in one platform. iVvy software includes tools for CRM, quoting, reporting, and real-time availability, helping teams speed up sales cycles, gain greater visibility into business operations, and maximise productivity.
What types of venues use iVvy?
iVvy is used by a wide range of venues, including hotels, function centres, restaurants, stadiums, universities, hospitality groups, and government agencies. It's ideal for any venue with multiple spaces that hosts regular events or functions.
The difference between iVvy and other event management software?
Unlike traditional platforms, iVvy combines venue management, booking, CRM, quoting, and reporting in one cloud-based system. It offers real-time availability, integrated payments, and tools to improve sales performance and operational efficiency.
Is iVvy cloud-based or installed locally?
iVvy is fully cloud-based, so you can access it from anywhere with an internet connection. There's no need for local installation or on-site servers.
Does iVvy integrate with accounting or property management systems?
Yes, iVvy integrates with leading accounting software and property management systems (PMS). These integrations allow seamless data flow between platforms, reduce manual entry, and improve operational efficiency for venues.
How does iVvy help streamline booking and quoting?
iVvy simplifies venue booking and quoting by offering real-time availability, customisable templates, and instant proposal generation. This helps venues respond to enquiries faster, reduce turnaround time, and improve quote accuracy.
Can I manage multiple venues with iVvy?
Absolutely. iVvy's multi-venue management software lets you oversee multiple properties from a single dashboard. It's ideal for hotel groups, clubs, and venue chains that need centralised control and reporting across multiple locations.
Is support actually responsive?
Yes. Teams are based across the UK, USA, Australia, South Africa, and Vietnam, so help is always available in your time zone. Average response time to connect with a real person is just 14.5 minutes. iVvy consistently achieves 90%+ customer satisfaction scores.
