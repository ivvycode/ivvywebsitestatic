Tilley & Wills

Now save 20+ hours a week on admin

Over the past eight years, Tilley & Wills has grown from a single landmark venue into a dynamic hospitality group with a portfolio that stretches across New South Wales and Queensland. What began with the Greenwood Hotel and Veranda Precinct has expanded to include Cabana Bar, Four Hundred, Annuka Beach Resort in Coffs Harbour, Oxford Art Factory, and Shaftston Hotel in Newcastle. Each venue has its own unique character, yet all share the same focus on creating exceptional guest experiences.

Problem

Before iVvy, event and catering management across the group was handled through a patchwork of manual processes. Run sheets were created in Word documents and had to be manually updated every time a client changed details. Bookings were tracked in spreadsheets, creating risks of double handling or errors. Invoices were generated manually, leaving room for costly mistakes.

The lack of a centralised system meant event information was scattered across documents, emails, and spreadsheets. Staff were constantly chasing updates, reconciling errors, and racing to stay on top of peak-season workloads.

"Everything was manual; Word documents, manual run sheets. It was an absolute nightmare to have to update those when a client would change numbers. And as you can imagine there were always a few financial hiccups manually generating invoices as well.", Saskia Eichler, Group Sales & Event Manager explained.

Without visibility into live availability, the team occasionally faced near-misses with double bookings. The result? High stress and inefficiencies that slowed down the team's ability to respond to enquiries and lock in new business.

iVvy Solution

With iVvy, Tilley & Wills have digitised their entire event management process, giving their team the speed, visibility and productivity to do more with less.

The team no longer needs to manually update run sheets or chase updates across spreadsheets. Every change is updated and shared instantly, keeping everyone on the same page from sales to operations.

Live availability and instant booking have replaced manual data entry, while a central function diary now displays all available and booked spaces across every Tilley & Wills property.

Invoices are generated automatically, reducing human error and ensuring clients receive accurate billing right away. iVvy's integration with SevenRooms takes visibility even further by automatically blocking out spaces for private events the moment they're booked.

Events with short lead times that once took hours to coordinate can now be organised in just 15 minutes.
