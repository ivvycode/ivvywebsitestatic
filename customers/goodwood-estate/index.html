Goodwood Estate

Streamlined Venue Management at Goodwood Estate with iVvy

Goodwood Estate, spanning 11,000 acres in the South Downs of West Sussex, has been the seat of the Dukes of Richmond for over three centuries. Renowned for its world-class reputation, the estate hosts three major sporting events annually: the Goodwood Festival of Speed, the Goodwood Revival, and the Qatar Goodwood Festival. The estate offers over a dozen versatile venues, including the grand Ballroom for large celebrations and the intimate Cedars Hall for smaller gatherings.

Background

Before integrating iVvy, Goodwood Estate had difficulty with an overly complicated and time-consuming sales process for both their team and clients. Coordinating between various venues was cumbersome, leading to inefficiencies and delays in the sales cycle.

The complexity of Goodwood's operations also made it difficult for the team to keep track of different events across multiple venue locations. The lack of streamlined communication between sales, planning, and operational teams often resulted in confusion and inefficiencies.

Dealing with the different needs of Goodwood's clients, especially international visitors who use various payment methods and have different VAT requirements, posed challenges in making sure everything was financially compliant.

iVvy Solution

iVvy's platform offers a centralised system for managing bookings and event schedules across Goodwood's various venues. This feature allows Goodwood to have a clear overview of all events taking place throughout the estate.

iVvy's fast quote generation and centralised lead management tools have given Goodwood a competitive edge by increasing the speed at which they can respond to enquiries.

With iVvy's CRM and vendor management features, Goodwood can oversee all vendor interactions and logistics from a single platform.

The software's integrated financial tools support various payment systems and compliance standards, helping Goodwood manage financial transactions smoothly.
